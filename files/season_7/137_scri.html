Teaser

INT. SUMMERS' HOUSE - LIVING ROOM - NIGHT

BUFFY enters the darkened living room, camera floating before her in a schmuck-baity kind of way. She shuts off the table lamp light, stops to pick something up.

We see it's a BOOK: "Cantonese For Beginners." Buffy looks at it, weary, and places it atop a STACK of books. "French For Beginners," "Speak Portuguese!" "Learn Swahili Now," "Spanish 101..."

We see the living room is filled with sleeping POTENTIALS. RONA on the sofa, AMANDA snuggled asleep in a chair, VI and CHAO-AHN on the floor. Buffy tiptoes between them, careful not to wake them, and, after looking them over like a watchful den mother, heads upstairs.

INT. SUMMERS' HOUSE - UPSTAIRS HALLWAY - NIGHT

Upstairs. Buffy gets to her bedroom door, opens it.

Buffy's POV: We see the room is FULL of Slayer POTENTIALS. MOLLY sleeping soundly in the bed. On the floor and around the room, THREE MORE girls sleep. All safe.

Buffy turns her head to look into Dawn's room, sees:

Buffy's POV: DAWN in her bed - and SIX Potentials (all non- speaking EXTRAS) packing the room, all asleep.

BUFFY shuts the door. She hears a low MOANING SOUND. Puzzled, she turns to see:

CHLOE, the Potential, sitting at the end of the hallway, in her slightly-young-looking JAMMIES, sitting huddled up against herself, hugging her knees, and CRYING.

BUFFY
Chloe?
(beat)
It is Chloe, right?

Buffy approaches Chloe, thinking of something to say. But not without suspicion as Chloe keeps crying.

When suddenly, a FLASH of motion smashes Buffy out of frame, VIOLENTLY.

ANGLE ON: THE STAIRS.

Buffy and her unseen ASSAILANT come CRASHING down the stairs together.

INT. SUMMERS' HOUSE FOYER - NIGHT

Buffy and her attacker land - WHAM! with a hard, bone rattling SLAM onto the foyer floor. Buffy gets PINNED down immediately, strong hands holding her shoulders tight.

Struggling, Buffy looks up to see: THE PRIMITIVE. Looming over her, peering into her eyes, intense.

PRIMITIVE
It's not enough.

And before Buffy can make sense of that we CUT TO:

INT. SUMMERS HOUSE - BUFFY'S BEDROOM - NIGHT

Buffy AWAKES with a start. Looks around. The room looks very much as it did in the dream - sleeping Potentials (FIVE of them, NONE of whom we have ever seen before) all around. Buffy gets her bearings, realizing where she is.

We hold on Buffy's face as her mind races, silently trying to figure out what the hell just happened...

END OF TEASER
