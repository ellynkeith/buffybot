Directly OFF THE BLACKESS, we see a large piece of BROKEN WOOD pulled away from frame, revealing Giles, Xander and Willow, removing wooden shards, looking down upon something within the wreckage...

XANDER
God almighty.

Reverse angle reveals: BUFFY, beaten, bloodied, lying like a broken doll in the wreckage.

DRUSILLA/FIRST (O.S.)
Do you know why you're alive?

INT. UNDERGROUND CAVERN - NIGHT

Drusilla stands before Spike, the seduction game pretty much over. Arms folded, stern, looking cross. Spike looks up at her, fakes a bloody grin.

SPIKE
Never figured you for...
(coughs)
... existential thought, love. I
mean, you hated Paris.

DRUSILLA/ FIRST
You're alive for one reason, and one
reason only.
(beat)
Because I wish it. Do you know why
I wish it? Because I'm not done with
you.

SPIKE
Give it up.
(spits)
Whatever you are - whatever you get
away with - I'm out. You can't pull
this puppet's strings anymore.

DRUSILLA/FIRST
And what makes you think you have a
choice? What makes you think you
will ever be any good at all in this
world?

SPIKE
She does.

Spike looks at her with simple resolve as he lays down his trump card.

SPIKE (cont'd)
Because she believes in me.

INT. SUMMERS' HOME - BUFFY'S ROOM - NIGHT

Buffy's FACE. Looking dead. Lifeless eyes. Completely and utterly defeated as we hear:

GILES (O.S.)
... her ribs are smashed, she should
be hospitalized immediately.

We push in, slowly...

WILLOW (O.S.)
Could she die?

GILES (O.S.)
Despite her healing abilities, I
believe so. I won't lie to you, I
don't know what to do in a situation
like this, because there has never
been a situation like this...

The voices fade as we keep moving in, almost floating as tears well up in her eyes, something within her swelling...

INT SUMMERS' HOME - DINING ROOM - NIGHT

Giles stands at the dining room table, addressing Willow, Dawn, Xander, Kennedy and Molly.

GILES
...which means we start back at
square one.

XANDER
Which square would that be, exactly?

GILES
I'm not sure. The First pre-dates
everything we've ever known, or can
know. It's everywhere. It's pure.
I don't know it we can fight it.

BUFFY (O.S.)
You're right.

All turn to see: BUFFY, standing in the dining room doorway. Bandaged and broken, but standing all the same.

BUFFY
We don't know how to fight it. We
don't know when it'll come. Can't
run, can't hide... can't pretend it's
not the end 'cause it is. Something
has always been there to try and
destroy the world. We've beaten them
back, but we're not dealing with them
anymore. We're dealing with the
reason they exist. Evil. The
strongest. The First.

GILES
Buffy, I know you're tired...

BUFFY
I'm beyond tired. I'm beyond scared.
I'm standing on the mouth of Hell and
it's going to swallow me whole.
(beat)
And it'll choke on me.

There is a fierce resolve creeping into her voice that surprises everyone. She takes a halting step forward.

BUFFY (cont'd)
We're not ready? THEY'RE not ready.
They think we're gonna wait, like we
always do, for the end to come but
I'm done waiting. They want an
apocalypse? We'll give 'em one.
Anyone else who wants to run do it
now, because we just became an army.
We just declared war.

We push in low on her -- and on everyone, as they are wrapped up in her passionate calm.

BUFFY (cont'd)
From now on we won't just face our
worst fears, we will seek them out.
We will find them and cut out their
hearts, one by one, until The First
shows itself for what it is. And I
will kill it myself. There's only
one thing on this earth more powerful
than evil. And that's us.

She looks around at them all.

BUFFY (cont'd)
Any questions?

BLACK OUT.

END OF SHOW
