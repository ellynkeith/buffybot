Teaser

EXT. SUNNYDALE CEMETERY - NIGHT

ON TWO PAIRS OF FEET. Running. We sense panic, and we're right. But as we pan up, we see the feet belong to TWO VAMPS. Two really scared vamps.

Behind them, three ATVs tear through the cemetery. Gaining. The vamps are the prey, and the hunters aboard the ATVS:

JONATHAN, WARREN and ANDREW.

And the guys are serious.

The ATVs are tricked out with long, pointy wooden stakes on the front. They actually are pretty menacing.

ANDREW
They're getting to the gate!

WARREN
I see it, I see it.

We see that the first vamp clutches in his hand a small, clear disk (as in round, not computer). The second vamp grabs a low-hanging branch from a tree, breaks it off, and tosses it behind him.

It knocks Andrew off his ATV, right in Warren's path. Warren swerves, bounces off a tombstone and CRASHES into Jonathan.

All three nerds sit up on the ground, dazed.

ANDREW
We're gonna lose them.

JONATHAN
We need that disk.

Warren watches as the vamps make a break for the cemetery gate. His eyes narrow... he sees something.

WARREN
Wait.

As the vamps round the gate, free at least, ready to roam wild in the big, bloody world, their relief is cut short.

BUFFY jumps in from the street. She TACKLES the vamps.

The disk goes skittering on the ground.

As Buffy faces off against the vamps, Warren watches, his face lighting up. He quietly moves forward, scoops up the disk and turns back to the other guys.

He motions for them to go.

Buffy has the First Vamp around the neck, trying to get a good position to stake him. The Second Vamp picks himself up off the street, CHARGES Buffy....

And gets SCOOPED into the air.

SPIKE stands atop the stone pillar of the cemetery gate, high above the ground, effortlessly dangling the Second Vamp mid-air by an arm. The vamp STRUGGLES to get free of Spike's grip.

Distracted, Buffy loses her grip on the First Vamp. He pushes her back, spins around and knocks her to the ground.

SPIKE
How you doin'?

BUFFY
(struggling)

SPIKE

if you want.

BUFFY

SPIKE

As Buffy rushes the First Vamp:

SPIKE

him down to you then?

Buffy tackles the First Vamp to the ground, gaining the upper hand. As she fights:

BUFFY

Rock the boat. Rock and roll it the
hell over. My friends and I'll still
be groovin' with the movin'.

She STAKES the First Vamp.

BUFFY (cont'd)

friends, my sister, last week and
guess how much they hate me now?
Zero. Zero much.

She gets up, walks over to Spike and the dangling Second Vamp.

BUFFY (cont'd)
So I'm thinking, sleeping with you...

She walks away. On her back...

BUFFY (cont'd)
They'll deal.

Spike watches her go then drops the Second Vamp. The vamp reacts with surprise at being free, then takes off running at Buffy's retreating back, fangs bared.

SPIKE
(calling after her)
In that case, why won't you sleep
with me again?

The Second Vamp can't help looking back at Spike at that. Buffy turns around, calmly STAKES the Second Vamp. She keeps walking away as he dusts, and says:

BUFFY
Because I don't love you.

On Spike. After a moment:

SPIKE
(to himself)
Like Hell.

INT. XANDER'S APARTMENT - NIGHT

The place feels big. Empty. A little unlived-in. XANDER sits on the floor, lost in the loneliness of the place, listening to lonely music on the stereo. Beer in one hand. Lost.

We move in, see he's in a great deal of pain. Missing Anya.

Finally, after a moment, he uses the remote to shut off the music. It stops abruptly, leaving us with uncomfortable, telling silence.

Another beat.

Xander stands up, sets down his beer, and walks to the door. He leaves...

EXT. XANDER'S APARTMENT - NIGHT

... and heads off into the night. Still with the lonely.

Across the street, a woman stands in the shadows, watching him go. When he's safely away, she steps into the light. ANYA. Human-looking. Conflicted. She wants to go after him. Instead, she just watches him leave.

BLACK OUT.

END OF TEASER
