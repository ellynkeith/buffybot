to piss me off, witch.

WILLOW

Students bustle. Buffy tears in with Dawn. She jostles students out of the way as she makes for the double doors on the other side.

Buffy and Dawn hit the double doors, escaping out of the building. BOOM! The opposite wall across the room explodes. Startled SCREAMS and STUDENTS DIVING FOR COVER.

Glory steps through the hole, snaps her eyes around he room. She becomes a RED BLUR as she switches into SPEED MODE. BOOM! The double doors leading outside are blown off their hinges as she exits.

(re: armor)
Shiny.

(to Dante)
